## üí° Content Writing Tips

### DO:
‚úÖ Use active voice ("We provide services" not "Services are provided")
‚úÖ Focus on impact and outcomes
‚úÖ Include specific examples and stories
‚úÖ Use inclusive, respectful language
‚úÖ Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences)
‚úÖ Use bullet points for lists
‚úÖ Write for your audience (donors, beneficiaries, partners)

### DON'T:
‚ùå Use jargon or acronyms without explanation
‚ùå Make claims you can't back up
‚ùå Use overly emotional or dramatic language
‚ùå Include outdated information
‚ùå Write in passive voice excessively
‚ùå Use all caps or excessive exclamation marks!!!
